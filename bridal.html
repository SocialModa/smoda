Luxury Bridal Gowns

Discover the epitome of bridal elegance with Social Moda's exclusive collection of couture wedding gowns. Each dress is a masterpiece of Turkish craftsmanship, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design. From the first sketch to the final stitch, our gowns are created to make you feel like the most beautiful version of yourself on your special day.

At Social Moda, we understand that your wedding dress is more than just a garment—it's a reflection of your personal style and the beginning of your love story. Our exclusive collection features handcrafted gowns made from the finest French laces, Italian silks, and delicate tulle. Each dress is designed to enhance your natural beauty and create unforgettable moments as you walk down the aisle. From the initial consultation to your final fitting, our expert stylists will guide you in finding the gown that speaks to your heart.

Signature Collections

Our signature collections are designed to inspire and enchant. The 'Timeless Elegance' line features classic silhouettes with modern twists, perfect for the traditional bride with a contemporary sensibility. The 'Modern Romance' collection showcases sleek, figure-hugging designs with unexpected details like illusion backs and delicate floral appliqués. For those seeking vintage charm, our 'Heritage' line revives classic designs with heirloom-quality craftsmanship and antique-inspired laces.

Each Social Moda gown is crafted using traditional Turkish embroidery techniques, with some pieces featuring up to 200 hours of hand-beading. We work with the finest European fabrics, including French Leavers lace, Italian silk mikado, and hand-dyed silks that drape like liquid. Our designers pay meticulous attention to every detail, from the placement of each crystal to the construction of the corset, ensuring both beauty and comfort throughout your special day.

Bespoke Design Service

For the bride seeking a truly one-of-a-kind creation, our bespoke design service brings your dream dress to life. Work directly with our master designers to create a gown that captures your vision down to the smallest detail. From initial sketches to fabric selection and custom embellishments, every element will be tailored to your preferences. Our atelier in Istanbul combines centuries-old craftsmanship with modern techniques to create a gown that is uniquely yours, perfectly fitted to your measurements and personal style.

Bridal Appointment Guide

Your bridal appointment at Social Moda is a special experience designed to help you find your dream dress. We recommend scheduling your first appointment 9-12 months before your wedding to allow time for ordering and alterations. Bring along your closest family or friends, and enjoy a glass of champagne as you say "yes" to the dress of your dreams.

Fabric Guide

Our gowns feature only the finest fabrics from Europe's most prestigious mills. Choose from luxurious silk mikado for structured silhouettes, delicate Chantilly lace for romantic detailing, or lightweight tulle for ethereal volume. Each fabric is carefully selected for its quality, drape, and ability to create the perfect silhouette for your special day.

Mermaid Silhouette

Our signature mermaid gowns are designed to accentuate your natural curves with a fitted bodice that flares at the knee. Featuring hand-embellished lace and delicate beading, these gowns create a dramatic, show-stopping silhouette perfect for the modern bride. Available in various necklines and sleeve options.

Ball Gown Collection

For the bride who dreams of a fairytale wedding, our ball gown collection features voluminous skirts and fitted bodices. Each gown is crafted with layers of tulle or organza, creating a magical, princess-worthy silhouette. The collection includes both traditional and contemporary designs, all with exquisite detailing and luxurious fabrics.

Minimalist Sheath

Our minimalist sheath dresses are perfect for the modern, sophisticated bride. Featuring clean lines, luxurious fabrics, and subtle details, these gowns are understated yet unforgettable. The simplicity of the silhouette allows for statement accessories or can stand alone in its elegant simplicity. Ideal for both traditional and contemporary wedding settings.
