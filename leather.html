Premium Leather Jackets

Discover the bold elegance of Social Moda's genuine lambskin leather jackets. Each piece is crafted with exceptional Turkish craftsmanship, blending tradition with contemporary design. Our leather jackets offer unmatched softness, durability, and timeless style — perfect for those who seek authenticity and refined edge.

Experience the epitome of style and craftsmanship with Social Moda's premium leather jackets. Each jacket is a testament to our commitment to excellence, combining traditional Turkish leatherworking techniques with contemporary designs. Whether you prefer the classic biker style, sleek moto jackets, or sophisticated bomber designs, our collection offers something for every taste and occasion.

Superior Craftsmanship

Every Social Moda leather jacket is handcrafted by master artisans in our Turkish ateliers. We use only the finest full-grain and top-grain leathers, carefully selected for their quality and durability. Our jackets feature reinforced stitching, premium hardware, and attention to detail that sets them apart. The natural variations in the leather ensure that each jacket is truly one-of-a-kind, developing a rich patina that tells your unique story over time.

Our commitment to quality extends to every aspect of production. From the initial cutting of the leather to the final quality inspection, each jacket undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards. We use eco-friendly tanning processes and source our materials responsibly, creating luxurious jackets that you can feel good about wearing.

Timeless Style & Versatility

A Social Moda leather jacket is more than just outerwear—it's a wardrobe essential that gets better with age. Our designs balance classic silhouettes with modern details, creating pieces that are both on-trend and timeless. Layer them over casual tees for a relaxed weekend look, or pair with tailored pieces for an elevated evening ensemble. With proper care, your leather jacket will become a cherished companion for years to come, developing character and softness that only comes with time and wear.

Classic Biker Jacket

Our signature biker jacket features a timeless design with asymmetrical zipper, wide lapels, and multiple pockets. Crafted from premium lambskin for exceptional softness and durability. Available in classic black, rich brown, and deep burgundy.

Modern Moto Jacket

Sleek and contemporary, our moto jacket features a streamlined silhouette with quilted detailing and zippered cuffs. Made from supple cowhide leather that molds to your body over time. Perfect for both casual outings and evening wear.

Bomber Jacket

A modern take on the classic bomber, featuring a lightweight construction with ribbed cuffs and hem. The perfect transitional piece that adds an edge to any outfit. Available in black and cognac finishes.

Why Choose Our Leather Jackets

Each Social Moda leather jacket is a masterpiece of Turkish craftsmanship, made from the finest lambskin and cowhide leathers. Our jackets are designed to age beautifully, developing a unique patina that tells your story. With attention to every detail, from the stitching to the hardware, our jackets offer unparalleled quality and style.
